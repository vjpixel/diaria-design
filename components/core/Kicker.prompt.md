**Kicker** — monospace, uppercase, wide-tracked eyebrow/meta label that sits above headings and along section edges.

```jsx
<Kicker accent>● Edição de hoje</Kicker>
<Kicker>Edição Nº 412 · Ter 19 Mai 2026</Kicker>
<Kicker onDark>Assine grátis</Kicker>
```

Use `accent` for the teal lead-in (often with a `●`), `onDark` on ink surfaces. Keep text short — it's a label, not a sentence.
